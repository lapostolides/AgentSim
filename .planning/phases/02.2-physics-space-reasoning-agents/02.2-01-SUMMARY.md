## Decisions Made
- Relationship dispatch table (dict of lambdas) chosen over if-elif for extensibility and clean separation
- Visited-edge tracking uses (input, output) tuple pairs to prevent cycles while allowing different paths to same output
- Max 100 BFS iterations as safety guard -- produces warning in PropagationResult.warnings if hit
- No eval() of formula strings -- formulas are documentation only, relationships are computed via dispatch table
